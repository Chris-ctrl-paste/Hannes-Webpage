  • Finland — Suomi laidasta laitaan Ten young photographers have travelled to the outer regions of Finland in order to pay tribute to the hundred years of Finland in this book. From the furthest to the south, north, west and east of the country, the book tells the story of the quietly impressive landscape of the country.

    Sofia Jern and Retta Tiainen — Texts
    Jonne Lehto — Finnish poems
    Ellen Strömberg — Swedish poems
    Johan Sandås — English translations
